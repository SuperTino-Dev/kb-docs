# Oracle EPM Drill-Through and Financial Traceability

Oracle Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) provides powerful drill-through capabilities that allow finance teams, auditors, and administrators to trace summarized balances back to their originating transactions.

<YouTube id="s2hoDmpD_7k" title="Oracle EPM Drill-Through and Financial Traceability" />

This capability improves:

- Financial transparency
- Audit readiness
- Reconciliation accuracy
- Month-end close efficiency
- Compliance and governance
